A Brief History of Cars

1886: The First Car

The first car was invented by Karl Benz in 1886. It was called the Benz Patent-Motorwagen and had a top speed of 10 mph.

1908: The Model T

The Model T, produced by Ford, was the first car to be mass-produced. It was affordable and reliable, making it accessible to the average person.

1973: The Oil Crisis

The Oil Crisis in the 1973 led to a shift in the car industry. Cars became more fuel-efficient and environmentally friendly.
