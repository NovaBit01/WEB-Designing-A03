History of Cars

Cars have been an essential part of our lives for over a century. Here is a brief history of cars:

  • 1886: The first carwas invented by Karl Benz in 1886. It was called the Benz Patent-Motorwagen and had a top speed of 10 mph.
  • 1908: The Model T, produced by Ford, was the first car to be mass-produced. It was affordable and reliable, making it accessible to the average person.
  • 1973: The Oil Crisis led to a shift in the car industry. Cars became more fuel-efficient and environmentally friendly.
